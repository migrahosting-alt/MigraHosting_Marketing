# MigraHosting Shared Hosting - Product Documentation

## Overview

MigraHosting is our shared web hosting product line, designed for personal sites, small businesses, and agencies. We offer four tiers: Student (free), Starter, Premium, and Business.

## Brand Positioning

- **Premium-but-Affordable**: Slightly cheaper than GoDaddy/HostGator/Bluehost, but with better tech (NVMe SSDs, dedicated mail server)

- **Security-First**: MigraGuard basic protection included (spam filtering, malware scanning, DDoS mitigation)
- **Real Support**: 24/7 human technicians + MigraAgent AI for instant help

## FAQ

### Do prices increase after renewal?
No. We show you the real renewal price upfront. If you sign up for 3-year Starter at $1.49/mo, you'll renew at $1.49/mo (subject to minor inflation adjustments, announced 60 days in advance).

### Is email included?
Yes! Every plan includes email hosting on our dedicated MigraMail server (not shared with web hosting). Student gets 1 mailbox, Starter gets 10, Premium gets 50, Business gets unlimited.

### Can I upgrade between plans?
Yes. Upgrade anytime from the mPanel dashboard. We'll pro-rate your current plan and apply the credit to the new one.

### Do you migrate my site for free?
Yes! Premium and Business plans include free migrations. Starter plans can add migration for $49 one-time. We handle everything: files, databases, emails, DNS.

### What's the refund policy?
30-day money-back guarantee on all plans (except Student, which is free). If you're not happy, we'll refund your hosting fee (domain registration fees are non-refundable per ICANN rules).

### Can I use my own domain?
Yes! All plans except Student support custom domains. You can transfer an existing domain or register a new one (free on yearly+ Starter+ plans).

### Is WordPress supported?
Absolutely. We're WordPress-optimized with 1-click installs, automatic updates, WP-CLI, Composer, and staging environments.
